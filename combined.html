Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972

Complete HR Compliance Guide + Advanced Calculator

Eligibility Rule

Eligible after completion of 4 years + 240 days of continuous service.

Exception: Death or Permanent Disablement – No minimum service required.

Gratuity Formula

(Basic + DA × 15 × Years of Service) ÷ 26
